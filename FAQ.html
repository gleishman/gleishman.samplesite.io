Frequently Asked Questions

Is your coffee roasted in house?
It sure is! We take a lot of pride in our custom roasts and are always looking for new ways to bring out hidden flavors.
Can I order a custom roast?
Yes, we will custom roast any order of 50 pounds or more.
Do you provide Baristas for catered events?
Yes, we have a mobile coffee shop/espresso bar that can be set up for any variety of events.
Do you offer franchise opportunities?
No, we are an independently owned and operated shop currently with two locations and a mobile truck.
Can I buy your coffee at any of the local grocery stores?
Currently no, but we're exploring opportunities with area farmers markets. If you don't live close to one of our two locations we're happy to ship our coffee right to your doorstep.
